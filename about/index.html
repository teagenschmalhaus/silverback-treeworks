Who We Are

About Silverback Treeworks

Silverback Treeworks is a locally owned tree service company that has been serving Greater Vancouver and the Sea to Sky corridor since 2007. We're not a franchise and we're not a call centre — we're a team of professionally trained arborists who take pride in doing the work right.

From high-canopy removals in North Vancouver's steep terrain to FireSmart clearances in Squamish, we bring the same ISA-certified expertise and safety standards to every job, regardless of size or location.

How We Work

What Sets Us Apart

✓
ISA Certified Arborists on Every Job Our arborists hold International Society of Arboriculture certification — the industry standard for tree care knowledge, safety, and ethics. You're not getting a labourer with a chainsaw; you're getting a trained professional who understands tree biology, risk assessment, and proper technique.
✓
Safety First, Every Time We are SAFE certified by the BC Forest Safety Council and in good standing with WorkSafeBC. Maintaining a safe workplace isn't a slogan — it's built into how we plan and execute every job. That protects our crew, your property, and your neighbours.
✓
Locally Owned and Operated We live and work in the communities we serve. We know the permit requirements in each municipality, the terrain challenges in each neighbourhood, and the tree species that define each part of the region. That local knowledge makes a real difference on complex jobs.
✓
Full-Service Capability From small ornamental pruning to large-scale crane removals, we have the equipment and crew to handle it. We don't subcontract the hard parts — our own certified team handles every stage of every job.
✓
Transparent Pricing Every quote is written, itemized, and provided before any work begins. No surprises on the invoice. We explain what we're recommending and why, so you can make an informed decision.
✓
Continuing Education The tree care industry evolves — new research on tree biology, updated safety standards, improved techniques. Our arborists stay current through ongoing ISA education requirements, ensuring we apply best practices on every job.
Credentials

Certified & Insured

✓
ISA — International Society of Arboriculture The globally recognized standard for professional arborist certification. ISA Certified Arborists pass rigorous exams covering tree biology, pruning standards, risk assessment, and safety protocols — and must maintain certification through ongoing education.
✓
SAFE Certified — BC Forest Safety Council SAFE certification demonstrates our commitment to a formal, documented safety management system. It's required by many strata councils, municipalities, and commercial clients — and it reflects how seriously we take crew and public safety.
✓
WorkSafeBC — Active & In Good Standing All Silverback Treeworks employees are covered under WorkSafeBC. We carry full liability insurance. Hiring an uninsured contractor for tree work puts you at significant legal and financial risk — we make sure that risk doesn't exist on our jobs.
