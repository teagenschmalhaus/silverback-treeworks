Common Questions

Tree Removal FAQ

Do I need a permit to remove a tree?

It depends on your municipality and the size of the tree. Most cities in Metro Vancouver and the North Shore require a permit for trees above a certain trunk diameter. We're familiar with the bylaws across all our service areas and can advise you during your free estimate — and assist with the permit application if needed.

How long does a tree removal take?

Most residential tree removals take half a day to a full day. Large, complex jobs — multiple trees, tight access, or crane work — may take two days. We'll give you a clear timeframe when we quote the job.

Do you remove the stump as well?

Stump removal is a separate service (stump grinding) but is commonly added to a removal in the same visit. Let us know when you request a quote and we'll include it. Grinding removes the stump to below grade so you can replant or lay sod over the area.

Can I keep the wood from my tree?

Absolutely. If you'd like logs for firewood or lumber, just let us know and we'll cut them to length and leave them on your property rather than chipping or hauling them away.
