Pemberton Tree Services

Pemberton's agricultural valley and mountain backdrop create a landscape unlike anywhere else in the Sea to Sky corridor. Cottonwood groves along the Lillooet River, mature conifers on the valley slopes, and the growing residential community in Pemberton Meadows and beyond all have distinct tree care needs — and not many certified arborists making the trip up to serve them.

Silverback Treeworks serves Pemberton and Mount Currie with the same full-service arborist capabilities we bring to every area we work. From hazardous tree removals to FireSmart assessments for interface properties, we make the trip and we bring the right equipment to get the job done properly.
