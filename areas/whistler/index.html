Whistler Tree Services

Whistler's forested mountain setting is part of what makes it one of the world's most desirable resort destinations — and it's also what makes tree care here uniquely complex. Dense lodgepole pine, Engelmann spruce, subalpine fir, and trembling aspen surround homes, chalets, and strata developments throughout the municipality. Many of these properties have strict RMOW tree management requirements.

Silverback Treeworks provides certified arborist services throughout Whistler, working with homeowners, strata councils, and property managers. We produce arborist reports that meet Resort Municipality of Whistler requirements and handle everything from deadwood removal and risk assessments to full tree removals and stump grinding.
