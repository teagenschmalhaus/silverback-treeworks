Richmond Tree Services

Richmond's flat terrain and mix of residential, agricultural, and commercial properties present a different set of tree care challenges than the North Shore mountains — but the need for professional, certified arborist service is just as real. From mature ornamental trees in established Steveston neighbourhoods to cottonwood and willow along the waterways, Silverback handles it all.

Richmond's tree bylaw requires permits for removal of protected trees on private property. We're familiar with the City's requirements and can prepare arborist reports, assess tree health, and recommend the right course of action for your property.
