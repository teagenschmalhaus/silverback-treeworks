Delta Tree Services

Delta encompasses three distinct communities — Ladner, Tsawwassen, and North Delta — each with its own character and tree care needs. North Delta has the most significant residential tree canopy, with mature Douglas fir and Big leaf maple on hillside lots. Ladner's waterfront and agricultural interface brings cottonwood and riparian species. Tsawwassen's established subdivisions are full of mature ornamental trees and hedges.

Silverback Treeworks serves all of Delta with the full range of tree care services. Whether you need a hazardous tree removed, a hedge program set up, or an arborist report for a permit, we're familiar with the Corporation of Delta's requirements and can get the job done right.
