Coquitlam Tree Services

Coquitlam's rapid residential expansion — particularly on Burke Mountain and Westwood Plateau — has created a patchwork of new homes adjacent to significant second-growth forest. Trees planted with homes in the 1980s and 90s are now reaching the size where professional arborist care becomes essential.

Silverback Treeworks handles everything from hazardous tree removals at the wildland-urban interface in Burke Mountain to routine pruning and stump grinding in Austin Heights and Maillardville. We're knowledgeable about the City of Coquitlam's tree protection bylaw and work with both residential and strata clients.
