# Silverback Treeworks — Current Website Analysis

Source: silverbacktreeworks.com (analyzed June 2026)

## What the Current Site Does Well

- Clear navigation: About, Services, Areas Served, Gallery, Testimonials, Contact
- ISA certification and SAFE Company badges are visible
- Phone number (604.312.7399) and "Request a Quote" CTA are prominent
- Educational resource articles exist (good for SEO)
- Lists 12+ served communities

## Improvement Areas for the New Version

### 1. Visual/Gallery — biggest gap
Only 3 photos for a company operating since 2007. A tree service is inherently visual. The new site should have a rich gallery with before/after shots categorized by service type. Existing video assets (`Silverback Website Video.mp4`, `Feb 27 video.mp4`) are not being used at all.

### 2. Location pages instead of a location list
The current site lists communities in one block. The new site needs dedicated pages for each service zone (Sea to Sky, Squamish/Whistler, North Vancouver/Vancouver/Burnaby) with zone-specific content. This is the primary SEO lever.

### 3. Social proof needs a lift
Testimonials exist as a page but lack trust signals. New site should display reviews with names, locations, and star ratings on the homepage — not just on a separate page.

### 4. Mobile-first design
Current site appears desktop-first. New version should be designed mobile-first since most quote requests come from phones.

### 5. Cleaner CTA strategy
"Request a Quote" appears inconsistently. Every page needs one clear primary action — a quote form or tap-to-call button on mobile.

### 6. Deeper service pages
Each service currently gets a brief blurb. Dedicated service pages with photos, pricing context, FAQs, and links to relevant location pages will improve both SEO and conversions.

### 7. Modern design refresh
Layout reads as cramped and dated. A cleaner, more spacious design with a strong green/natural color palette, bold typography, and full-width hero imagery would better match what customers expect from a premium tree service.
