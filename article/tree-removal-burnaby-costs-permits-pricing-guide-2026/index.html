Tree Removal Burnaby: Costs, Permits & Pricing Guide 2026

Tree Removal Burnaby, BC Silverback Treeworks

Pricing for tree removal in Burnaby covers everything from the initial assessment through cutting, cleanup, and disposal. This guide gives you a realistic picture of what to budget — based on actual crew day rates, tree size categories, and the permit fees Burnaby charges.

What Does Tree Removal Cost in Burnaby?

Silverback Treeworks' day rate for a three-person crew with truck and chipper is approximately $3,000. This provides a useful baseline for a full day's work. Most standard residential removals complete within a single day.

Spider lift services, when needed for access, add approximately $600.

Key Cost Drivers

  • Tree size and height: determines crew size and equipment requirements
  • Accessibility: tight yard access, proximity to structures, and steep terrain (common in Burnaby Mountain and South Burnaby)
  • Tree condition: dead or diseased trees are more unpredictable and require slower, more careful work
  • Permit requirements: arborist reports add $150–$300; permit fees are charged by the city

Burnaby Permit Requirements

Burnaby requires a Tree Cutting Permit for any tree with a diameter greater than 20 cm (8 inches), measured 1.4 metres above the ground. Applications need site plans, photographs, measurements, and sometimes an arborist report.

Silverback Treeworks handles the full permit process for Burnaby clients — arborist report preparation, application submission, and follow-up with the city.

Additional Cost Items

  • Stump grinding: $150–$500 depending on diameter — we recommend doing it at the time of removal
  • Wood chipping and hauling: typically included in our quotes
  • Emergency callouts: after-hours and storm-response work carries a premium

Getting an Accurate Quote

The most reliable way to get an accurate estimate is a free on-site visit. We can also often provide a ballpark quote from a short video of the tree — contact us and we'll advise the best approach for your job.
