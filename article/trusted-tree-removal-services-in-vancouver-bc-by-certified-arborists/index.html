Why Professional Tree Removal Matters in Vancouver

Professional tree removal in Vancouver combines certified expertise, safety protocols, and local knowledge of the city's bylaws and terrain. The core advantages: ISA Certified Arborist oversight, advanced equipment, full liability coverage, efficient cleanup, and permit handling that keeps you on the right side of Vancouver's Protection of Trees Bylaw.

How ISA Certified Arborists Ensure Safe Removal

ISA Certified Arborists don't just show up with chainsaws. Before a cut is made, they conduct detailed risk assessments, design rigging and section-felling plans, and establish safe exclusion zones. They follow ANSI Z133 safety standards for fall protection and chainsaw use. On large or complex jobs, this planning phase is what prevents property damage and injury.

Common Reasons for Tree Removal in Vancouver

  • Safety hazards — dead limbs, compromised structure, or lean toward structures
  • Storm damage or sudden structural failure
  • Disease or pest infestation that can't be treated
  • Construction and property development
  • Space reclamation for landscaping or building

When to Remove vs. When to Treat

Removal is not always the right answer. Removal is necessary when visible decay (fungal conks or cavities exceeding 30% of the trunk), structural failures (splits or cracks in the main scaffold), or pest infestations that have progressed past treatment thresholds are present. An arborist assessment gives you an honest picture of your options before any work is committed to.

Permit Requirements in Vancouver BC

Any tree with a diameter of 20 cm or greater at 1.4 m above the ground is protected under Vancouver's tree bylaw. Permit required before removal. Unauthorized removal carries fines of $750–$5,000 per tree.

We prepare the arborist report, submit the application, and follow up with Urban Forestry on your behalf.
