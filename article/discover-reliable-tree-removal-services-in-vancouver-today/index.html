Tree Removal Costs in Vancouver

Tree removal pricing in Vancouver varies widely — from small backyard trees to massive, hard-to-reach specimens, every job is different. Our day rate for a three-person crew with truck and chipper is in the $3,000 range, with job duration determined by tree size, access, and condition. That rate covers assessment, cutting, cleanup, and disposal.

What Drives Tree Removal Prices

  • Tree size and species: larger, denser trees take more crew time and equipment
  • Site access: tight yards, proximity to structures, or no driveway access
  • Tree condition: dead or diseased trees require more careful, slower removal
  • Permit fees: Vancouver requires permits for protected trees, which adds $108–$310+ per tree
  • Emergency service: after-hours storm response carries a premium

Vancouver Tree Removal Permits

Vancouver's Protection of Trees Bylaw 9958 protects trees on private property with trunks 20 cm or wider in diameter (measured 1.4 m above the ground). This regulation supports Vancouver's goal of increasing tree canopy cover from 18% to 30% by 2030.

When You Need a Permit

  • Tree is 20 cm or larger in diameter
  • Tree is in a protected zone or designated greenway
  • Removal affects public right-of-way or city-owned land

Penalties for Removing Without a Permit

Fines range from $750 to $5,000 per tree, plus potential replanting requirements. Silverback Treeworks handles the full permit application process — arborist report, application submission, and follow-up with City Urban Forestry.

Why Hire an ISA Certified Arborist

ISA Certified Arborists have passed rigorous exams covering tree biology, safe removal techniques, risk assessment, and local bylaw knowledge. Hiring a certified arborist protects you from unsafe work, improper cuts, and the liability that comes with a botched removal near your home.

Silverback Treeworks has ISA Certified Arborists on every job — not just on the estimate visit.

Emergency Tree Removal in Vancouver

We provide 24/7 emergency response throughout Greater Vancouver and the Sea to Sky corridor. If a tree fails during a storm or becomes an immediate hazard, call us at 604.312.7399. We aim to arrive within two hours.

Signs you may need emergency removal:

  • Major cracks or splits in the trunk or main scaffold branches
  • Tree leaning noticeably more than before
  • Rot, fungus, or visible decay near the base
  • Uprooted root systems or soil heaving around the base
  • Branches in contact with power lines

Residential vs. Commercial Tree Removal

Residential jobs prioritize protecting lawns, driveways, and garden areas — and minimizing disruption to neighbours. Commercial projects typically involve larger trees, more complex permits, insurance documentation, and working around business operating hours. We handle both with the same full-service approach.
